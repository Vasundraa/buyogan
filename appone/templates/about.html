We are Buyoganic

"We are a dedicated team of individuals committed to making a positive impact on our environment. Allow us to introduce ourselves: Rishi S, Vasundraa S, and Selvapriyadharshini S. We embarked on this project during a hackathon event, driven by our collective passion to address the critical issue of single-use plastic and its detrimental impact on our surroundings. Our mission is to raise awareness and provide practical solutions to reduce single-use plastic consumption by promoting the adoption of efficient alternative materials. With this goal in mind, we have developed this webpage to offer valuable insights into the environmental degradation caused by single-use plastics and empower individuals with sustainable choices. We have poured our hearts and souls into creating this initial version of the webpage you see before you, investing nearly six months of dedicated effort. As first-year, second-semester students pursuing our Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering at Dr. N.G.P. Institute of Technology, we are driven by a shared determination to make a difference. Through our combined skills, knowledge, and unwavering commitment, we aim to make a meaningful impact on our environment. Join us on this journey towards a greener, more sustainable future. Together, we can work towards reducing single-use plastic consumption and creating a healthier planet for generations to come. Thank you for visiting our webpage and supporting our cause..
